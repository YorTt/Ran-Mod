History=First entering the Royal Australian Navy service in 1969, the Oberon-class was designed to be a bigger more and more capable version of it's predocessor, the Porpoise-class. A total of 27 boats where built, and saw service with 5 different countries around the world. A total of eight 21" torpedo tubes where mounted in the original design, 6 forward and 2 non-reloadable tubes aft. The Royal Australian Navy operated 6 boats of this class.
